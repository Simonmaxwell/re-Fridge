re:Fridge
Welcome to re:Fridge. This is a tool to help you clean out your fridge by finding you recipes that make use of any troublesome ingredients you just can't seem to get rid of. Start by adding ingredients via the top input field and the plus sign. If you're picky you can also add ingredients you don't want to show up in your recipes with the bottom field and the minus sign. When you've created your lists just hit the search button to see your recipes.
